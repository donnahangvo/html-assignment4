Events and Activites

Explore the River Stride Trail with a scavenger hunt designed to get you to open your eyes to the beauty around you. This event is open to all participants. Groups of 4 participants will be formed to make teams to make the treasure hunt exciting. Follow specially marked trails for best scavenger hunt results.

Celebrate the beauty of Greenscape with an outdoor barbacue and fun activities. Become one with nature and make new friends by partying the day away with a barbacue and fun activites to get in touch with the beauty of nature at Greenscape National Park. Located in the parking lot of the Mountain Ascend Trail near the base of the mountain.

Learn how to forage for edible plant life, lead by our expert forager. Explore the edible offerings of nature in the Forest Meander Trail, and learn about wild plant life that can offer a bountiful harvest if you look in the right places.
